Work Together Well
  1. Short term goal: Develop an online course to help people learn how to be a valued member of a great team.
  2. Long term goal: Build a better learning platform and open it up to other teachers.

Key concepts: Capability, Capability Map, Learning Story, Learning Journey

Design principles for our learning platform:

  • Design around CAPABILITIES (defined by mastery criteria). Be explicit and transparent about them.
  • Keep the capabilities highly granular to allow for personalisation, incremental learning and insights into the learning process.
  • Collect capabilities into a CAPABILITY MAPS to show the big picture. Allow teachers and to craft LEARNING JOURNEYS through the Map.
  • Focus on doing to develop and demonstrate MASTERY rather than consuming content on the topic.
  • Support a learning process that includes review and reinforcement not just one time learning.

Regular eLearning and typical live workshops suck:

  • focused on content consumption not developing competencies ~> we help you develop specific competencies
  • one time experiences ~> we guide you through a learning journey so that you can internalise key skills
  • one size fits all ~> we let you decide which areas to focus on
  • poor retention and follow through ~> we translate knowledge into action-oriented mastery criteria
